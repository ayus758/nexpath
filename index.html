Real Interview Experiences

People who cleared exams and interviews share exactly what happened — questions asked, preparation strategy, and honest advice.

RS
Rahul Sharma Google SWE L3
Campus → Google · 3 rounds · Selected ✓ · 6 months prep
My Google interview had 3 technical rounds. Round 1 was pure DSA — two medium LeetCode problems in 45 mins. Round 2 focused on system design (design a URL shortener). Round 3 was behavioral + system design. They care a lot about communication, not just the answer — practice thinking out loud. I solved 280 problems before the interview and revised system design from Grokking the System Design Interview.

Priya Kapoor GATE CSE 2024
GATE 2024 · AIR 187 · Score: 78.5 · 8 months prep
For GATE I focused on standard textbooks and previous year papers. The key is to not skip Mathematics — it carries 15% weightage. I made handwritten notes for every subject and revised them 3 times. Started 8 months before the exam. GO book for Computer Networks, CLRS for Algorithms. Mock tests every weekend in the final 2 months helped me manage time pressure during the actual exam.
